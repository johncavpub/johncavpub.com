The clue is simple: *Where the meadow meets the hill.* The mission? Anything but.

Riddler Rabbit is back — and this time he's trading the schoolyard for the shadows. Armed with a secret map, a trusty satchel, and one very opinionated parrot, Riddler must crack a trail of cryptic clues before someone else gets there first. The target looms on a hilltop behind locked gates and searchlights, and the rain isn't slowing down for anyone.

This isn't the timid bunny you remember. Riddler 3 is a pulse-pounding, code-cracking adventure for young readers ready to level up — full of mystery, daring escapes, and the kind of riddles that will keep you guessing until the very last page.
