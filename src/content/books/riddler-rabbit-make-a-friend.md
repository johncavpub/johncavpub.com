Riddler Rabbit is excited to start his first day of school, but when he starts to feel anxious about making new friends, he faces a challenge he wasn't expecting: a panic attack. With the help of his new friend, Pecky the Parrot, Riddler learns that it's okay to feel scared and learns how to calm down when worry takes over.

Riddler discovers that with a kind friend by his side, even the scariest moments can be faced with courage. _Riddler Rabbit Makes a Friend_ is a heartwarming story about friendship and managing anxiety that raises awareness and teaches coping skills to young readers.
